Our Services

Food Catering

To provide quality and a wider range of cuisine while travelling , IRCTC has introduced its new eCatering service for food delivery in train. Available for download from Google Play and iTunes you can now easily install the app on your android or iOS and get palatable yet cheap food delivery in train from a variety of food partners you can choose from.

Group Order

Passengers willing to get food delivered via IRCTC eCatering for a group of 15 or more members can share their details to get customised menu. Our customers suppport executive will call you back with the best offers.

Food Ordering

With this new online food delivery service passengers can order food on train by simply entering their travel details such as PNR number , Train name , seat/berth number and expect a delicious and quick food delivery on train.This effective system of online food delivery on train not only guarantees tasty food on your seat but also a revamped railway food menu making yor journey a bit more fun.
